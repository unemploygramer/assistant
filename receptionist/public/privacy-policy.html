Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 19, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use our voicemail and phone receptionist service, we collect:

  • Call Data: Phone numbers (caller and recipient), call duration, call recordings/transcripts, and call metadata
  • Business Information: Business name, calendar ID, and configuration preferences you provide
  • Lead Information: Information provided during calls, including names, phone numbers, addresses, and service requests
  • Account Information: Email address and authentication credentials for dashboard access

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Process and route incoming phone calls
  • Generate call transcripts and summaries
  • Create and manage leads from phone conversations
  • Send notifications (email and SMS) about new leads and appointments
  • Provide customer support and improve our services

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (database) and Twilio (call processing). We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. Call transcripts and lead data are retained as long as your account is active or as required by law.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Twilio: For phone call processing and SMS delivery
  • Supabase: For data storage and authentication
  • OpenRouter/OpenAI: For AI-powered call handling
  • ElevenLabs: For text-to-speech audio generation
  • Google Calendar: For appointment scheduling (if configured)

These services have their own privacy policies. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of SMS notifications by replying STOP
  • Request a copy of your data

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at: codedbytyler@gmail.com

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
